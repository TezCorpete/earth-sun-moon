What Causes The Seasons?

It takes the earth about 365 days to orbit the sun in a counter-clockwise direction and with this we get seasons. When the Earth orbits around the Sun is does not move it only rotates so both the north and south hemispheres will always face the same direction in space. This means that when one hemisphere is getting more direct sunlight the other isnt (this is becuase the earth is titled).
