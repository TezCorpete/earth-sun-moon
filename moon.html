The Moon


Moon orbit

The Moon orbits the Earth in a counter-clockwise direction. The reason the Moon orbits the Earth is because the gravity of the Earth keeps it in orbit, and inertia keeps it moving.


Lunar Cycle

The lunar cycle is the procces of one moon phase showing up again or the moon orbiting the earth. The moon has 8 phases with the new moon representing the start of the cycle or a new month (the lunar cycle is about 30 days). During the Full Moon the light of the Sun is visble on the entire visible Moon and during the New Moon it isnt becuase light is on the side of the Moon we cant see (facing the sun). The Moon phases labeled waxing and the 1st quarter are going towards a full moon or more light and the phases labeled waning and 3rd quarter are going towards a new moon or less light (wax on wan off).


Phenonmnons envolving the moon

Solar Eclipse

When the Moon is in between the Earth and the Sun therefore casting a shadow on the Earth

Lunar Eclipse

When the Earth is bewteen the Moon and Sun therefore casting a shadow on the Moon
