Gravitys Role In The Universe

Gravity Brings Everything Together

Gavity: the force that attracts a body toward the center of the earth, or toward any other physical body having mass.

Because of gravity, things are pulled towards each other no matter how far. This is affected by mass and distance. If there are two objects the closer or the higher one or both objects are make the force of gravity higher. Gravity also is the reason that the moon orbits the earth it is a smaller object and the earth's gravity causes the moon to orbit it
